About

About me and this site

Hello! My name is John Boyd. I am an electrical engineer by trade and a hacker at heart. I worked in the semiconductor industry for a few years as an analog & mixed signal design engineer, but lately my career has followed my passion for embedded systems design. I now work as CTO and lead hardware engineer at Knocki, a fun startup trying new ideas in the IoT space.

I have created this blog as a place to document my journeys through technology development and hardware hacking. I am not sure what projects will make it into this blog, but any of the following subjects are likely to make an appearance:

  • Embedded Systems Design
    • Using C/C++ or Rust
    • For ARM, MSP430, Atmel/AVR, and other MCUs
  • PCB Design, Schematic Capture, Layout & Fabrication
  • Machine Learning
  • Signal Processing (analog, digital & mixed)
  • VLSI Design
  • Reverse Engineering
  • Mathematics
  • 3D Printing

I hope you enjoy these projects as much as I do!
